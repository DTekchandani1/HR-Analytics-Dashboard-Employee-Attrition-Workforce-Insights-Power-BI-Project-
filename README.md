HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights (Power BI Project)
1.	Problem Objective
To Help an organization to improve employee performance and improve employee retention 
(reduce attrition) by creating a HR Analyst dashboard. The dashboard provides actionable insights into employee turnover, salary distribution, demographics, and job role impact — enabling HR teams and business leaders to make data-driven retention and workforce planning decisions.


2.  Business Problem
Employee attrition significantly impacts organizational cost, productivity, and morale.
This dashboard aims to:
•	Identify key drivers behind employee attrition
•	Analyze attrition by age, salary, education, and job role
•	Provide HR decision-makers with interactive workforce insights
•	Support data-driven retention strategies

13.  Business Recommendations
•	Improve Early Employee Retention: Strengthen onboarding and mentorship programs to reduce attrition among employees with fewer years at the company.
•	Review Compensation Strategy: Reassess salary structure for lower pay bands and introduce performance-based incentives to improve retention.
•	Focus on High-Attrition Roles: Implement targeted engagement and workload management strategies for Sales and Research roles.
•	Enhance Career Growth Opportunities: Provide clear promotion paths, skill development programs, and internal mobility options to retain mid-career employees.
These actions can help reduce attrition, improve employee satisfaction, and lower hiring and training costs.
